You've heard about the "Internet of things." But what exactly are these "things," and what are they good for? More importantly, how can you hack them to do things that smarter objects just can't handle? In this story, we track devices that don't need a touchscreen or a fancy enclosure to make intelligent use of wireless networking, sensors, servos, Arduinos and other physical computing components. Plus we'll keep tabs on the people developing killer projects with them, and what these devices might mean for tomorrow's software developers.
