Bitcoin, the digital decentralized currency, was introduced in 2009 by an anonymous developer who goes by the pseudonym Satoshi Nakamoto. The untraceable currency became notorious for its early use as the online gray market's money of choice, but in recent months it's gained legitimacy as more marketplaces, vendors, and governments have awoken to its existence. The advantage: Bitcoin is nearly impossible for sovereignties to tax. Here we are tracking Bitcoin with a synthesis of news from around the web.
