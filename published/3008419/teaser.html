It’s perhaps the most critical step in user adoption: the First-Time User Experience, or FTUE. The FTUE takes many forms. It can be the initial setup screen requesting users enter information that will help make their experience better (think, Quora asking you which subjects interest you most when you first sign up); it can be the overlays describing what the various buttons do that appear the first time you launch an app; it can be the pairing process between a user’s smartphone and your DVR product.
